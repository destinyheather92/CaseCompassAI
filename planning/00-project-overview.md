# CaseCompass AI — Project Overview

## Project Summary

CaseCompass AI is an AI-powered legal research roadmap platform designed to help incarcerated individuals understand where to begin researching their legal questions. Rather than providing legal advice, CaseCompass AI transforms plain-language questions into personalized legal research roadmaps that guide users toward relevant legal concepts, statutes, case law, and educational resources.

The platform bridges the gap between having access to a prison law library and actually understanding how to use it. By combining guided intake, plain-language explanations, AI-assisted legal education, and structured research plans, CaseCompass AI empowers users to research their own cases with greater confidence and direction.

---

# Mission

To improve access to justice by helping incarcerated individuals understand what to research, why it matters, and where to begin.

---

# Vision

To become the leading AI-powered legal education and research guidance platform for correctional facilities, legal aid organizations, prison education programs, and individuals navigating the justice system.

Our long-term vision is to ensure that no one loses the opportunity to understand their legal rights simply because they lack legal training or cannot afford an attorney.

---

# Problem Statement

Millions of incarcerated individuals have access to prison law libraries but struggle to use them effectively.

The challenge is rarely a lack of information—it is a lack of direction.

Users often begin with questions such as:

- Can I appeal my conviction?
- Was my sentence calculated correctly?
- What does this court opinion mean?
- Did my attorney make a mistake?
- What should I research first?

Most users have limited legal literacy and are expected to navigate thousands of pages of statutes, appellate opinions, procedural rules, and legal terminology without knowing where to begin.

Traditional legal research tools assume users already understand legal concepts.

CaseCompass AI removes that barrier.

---

# Solution

CaseCompass AI asks users guided questions in plain language and builds a personalized legal research roadmap based on their circumstances.

Instead of overwhelming users with search results, the platform helps them:

- Understand legal terminology
- Identify relevant legal issues
- Discover applicable statutes
- Locate related case law
- Learn procedural requirements
- Build an organized research plan
- Continue researching with confidence

The application is educational in nature and does not provide legal advice.

---

# Product Principles

CaseCompass AI should always be:

### Educational

The application teaches users how legal research works.

### Transparent

AI responses should clearly explain how conclusions were reached and provide citations whenever possible.

### Accessible

Content should be understandable at approximately a 6th-grade reading level whenever practical.

### Trustworthy

The platform should avoid speculation, unsupported claims, or misleading confidence.

### Privacy Focused

User information should be handled securely and never shared without consent.

### Human-Centered

The experience should reduce intimidation and encourage confidence while acknowledging the complexity of the legal system.

# Non-Goals

CaseCompass AI does **not**:

- Provide legal advice
- Replace licensed attorneys
- Predict case outcomes
- Guarantee legal success
- Draft court filings for submission
- Represent users in legal matters

The platform exists solely as an educational legal research assistant.
