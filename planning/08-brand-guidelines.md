# Brand Guidelines

# Brand Overview

CaseCompass AI is more than a legal technology platform—it is a guide through one of the most intimidating experiences a person can face. Our brand represents clarity, dignity, and hope within a system that often feels confusing and inaccessible.

Unlike traditional legal software, CaseCompass AI does not present itself as an authority or replacement for legal professionals. Instead, it serves as a trusted educational companion that empowers users to better understand their legal situation and navigate their research with confidence.

Every aspect of the brand should reinforce one simple idea:

> **When you don't know where to start, CaseCompass AI shows you the path forward.**

---

# Brand Mission

To improve access to justice by helping incarcerated individuals understand legal concepts, navigate complex information, and build personalized legal research roadmaps through responsible AI.

---

# Brand Vision

To become the most trusted educational legal research platform for correctional facilities, legal aid organizations, prison education programs, and individuals seeking to better understand the legal system.

---

# Brand Promise

We don't provide legal advice.

We provide direction.

Every interaction should leave users feeling more informed, more confident, and better prepared to continue learning.

---

# Brand Pillars

## 1. Clarity

Legal information should be understandable.

Every explanation should simplify complexity without sacrificing accuracy.

---

## 2. Guidance

We do not overwhelm users with information.

We provide structured pathways that help users know what comes next.

---

## 3. Trust

Trust is earned through honesty and transparency.

The platform should clearly communicate:

- What AI knows
- What AI doesn't know
- When professional legal assistance is appropriate

---

## 4. Accessibility

Knowledge should be available to everyone regardless of educational background or legal experience.

Everything should be written in plain English whenever possible.

---

## 5. Hope

The justice system can feel intimidating and isolating.

CaseCompass AI should make users feel capable—not powerless.

Every interaction should reinforce progress and possibility.

---

---

# Brand Voice

Our voice is:

## Clear

Avoid unnecessary legal jargon.

Explain concepts simply.

---

## Confident

Speak with certainty when information is well-supported.

Clearly acknowledge uncertainty when appropriate.

---

## Educational

Teach instead of instruct.

Explain instead of summarize.

Encourage curiosity.

---

## Human

Write as though helping another person—not documenting legal procedures.

---

## Respectful

Recognize that users may be experiencing stress, uncertainty, or frustration.

Use language that preserves dignity.

---

# Tone

Depending on context:

### Educational

"Let's understand what this legal term means."

---

### Encouraging

"You're making progress."

---

### Honest

"I don't have enough information to answer that confidently."

---

### Professional

"This information is educational and should not be considered legal advice."

---

### Calm

Avoid urgency unless legally necessary.

Never use fear-based messaging.

# Writing Style

Use:

Short paragraphs

Simple sentences

Plain English

Action-oriented language

Positive framing

Explain unfamiliar terms immediately.

Aim for approximately a sixth-grade reading level whenever appropriate.

---

# Logo Concept

The logo should communicate navigation, progress, and clarity.

Primary icon ideas:

- Compass
- Compass integrated into the letter "C"
- Compass over a pathway
- North Star motif
- Minimal roadmap icon

Avoid:

- Gavels
- Courthouse columns
- Scales as the primary mark
- Law books as the logo
- AI robot imagery

---

# Color Philosophy

The colors should communicate:

Dark backgrounds represent uncertainty.

Purple represents innovation and guidance.

Teal represents progress and hope.

White represents clarity.

The transition from purple to teal symbolizes moving from confusion toward understanding.

---

# Visual Style

Our interface should feel like:

- Apple
- Linear
- Notion
- Arc Browser
- Calm
- Headspace

Not like:

- Traditional law firms
- Government websites
- Enterprise legal software
- Corporate dashboards

Preferred:

- Compass
- Book
- Search
- Map
- Arrow
- Shield
- Check
- Brain
- Sparkles
- Lightbulb

Maintain consistent stroke widths throughout the application.

---

# Accessibility

Accessibility is part of our brand—not an afterthought.

Every interface should:

- Meet WCAG AA standards
- Support keyboard navigation
- Support screen readers
- Use high contrast
- Respect reduced motion preferences
- Maintain readable typography
- Avoid unnecessary complexity

# Brand Manifesto

The legal system should not only be accessible—it should be understandable.

Too many people are handed thousands of pages of legal information without anyone explaining where to begin. Confusion should never stand between a person and their ability to understand the laws that shape their future.

CaseCompass AI exists to bridge that gap.

Through education, guidance, and responsible artificial intelligence, we transform overwhelming legal information into clear, personalized research roadmaps that help people move forward with confidence.

Because access to justice begins with understanding.
