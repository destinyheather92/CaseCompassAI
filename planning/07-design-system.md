# Design System

# Overview

The CaseCompass AI Design System establishes a consistent visual language that communicates trust, clarity, innovation, and hope. Unlike traditional legal software, the interface should not feel cold, corporate, or intimidating. Instead, every interaction should reinforce the idea that users are moving from confusion toward understanding.

The design language is inspired by Apple's elegance, Linear's modern UI, Notion's simplicity, and the calm, focused experience of premium productivity applications.

---

# Design Principles

Every interface should communicate:

- Clarity over complexity
- Guidance over information overload
- Confidence over intimidation
- Accessibility over decoration
- Premium craftsmanship
- Modern justice technology
- Calm and focused interactions

Users should never feel overwhelmed by the interface itself.

# UX Philosophy

Every screen should answer three questions:

1. What am I looking at?
2. Why does this matter?
3. What should I do next?

If a component doesn't help answer one of these questions, it should be simplified or removed.

---

# Design Statement

CaseCompass AI is more than a legal research platform—it's a guide. Every color, animation, interaction, and layout decision should reinforce the feeling that the user is moving from uncertainty toward clarity. The interface should communicate hope without sacrificing professionalism, creating an experience that is memorable, trustworthy, and distinctly different from traditional legal software.
