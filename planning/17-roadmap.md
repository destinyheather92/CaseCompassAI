# 17. Project Roadmap

## Vision

The development roadmap for CaseCompass AI is designed to transform an initial proof of concept into a secure, scalable, institution-ready platform that improves access to legal understanding for incarcerated individuals.

Development will follow an iterative approach, allowing features to be validated through user feedback while minimizing technical debt and ensuring the platform remains maintainable as it grows.

---

# Guiding Principles

Every phase of development should prioritize:

- Accessibility
- Simplicity
- Security
- Educational value
- AI transparency
- Scalability
- Institutional readiness

Each milestone should deliver meaningful value while laying the foundation for future enhancements.

---

# Phase 1 — Foundation

### Objective

Build a polished MVP demonstrating the core CaseCompass experience.

### Goals

- Design responsive landing page
- Authentication system
- User dashboard
- Guided legal intake
- Personalized research roadmap
- Plain-language legal translator
- Legal term explainer
- Basic AI integration
- Research history
- Responsive UI
- Initial branding

### Deliverables

- Landing Page
- Dashboard
- Guided Intake
- AI Roadmap Generator
- Authentication

# Success Metrics

The roadmap will be considered successful if CaseCompass AI can:

- Help users identify what legal topics to research.
- Explain complex legal concepts in plain language.
- Reduce barriers caused by low legal literacy.
- Deliver citation-backed educational content.
- Minimize AI misinformation through RAG and validation.
- Support secure institutional deployment.
- Scale to correctional facilities, educational programs, and legal aid organizations.
- Become a trusted educational platform that empowers users to better understand the legal system while recognizing that it does not replace licensed legal counsel.
