# Landing Page Specification

# Overview

The CaseCompass AI landing page serves one purpose:

**Convince visitors within five seconds that this platform solves a problem no other legal research tool addresses.**

Unlike traditional legal software that focuses on search or document management, CaseCompass AI immediately communicates that it provides **direction**. The landing page should create an emotional connection by acknowledging the confusion many incarcerated individuals face and presenting a clear path toward understanding.

The page should feel premium, cinematic, modern, and memorable—more like an Apple product launch than a law firm's website.

---

# Primary Goal

Guide visitors toward one action:

> **Build My Research Roadmap**

Everything on the page should reinforce why that action matters.

# Visual Style

The landing page should feel:

- Premium
- Cinematic
- Calm
- Purpose-driven
- Modern
- Minimal
- Inspiring

Avoid:

- Generic SaaS layouts
- Bright white backgrounds
- Law firm aesthetics
- Government styling
- Excessive gradients
- Clutter

---

# Motion Design

Use Framer Motion.

Animations include:

- Hero fade-in
- Roadmap line drawing
- Sequential roadmap nodes
- Statistic count-up
- Card hover elevation
- Button glow
- Smooth section reveals

Animations should reinforce progress, never distract.

---

# Accessibility

The landing page must:

- Meet WCAG AA standards
- Support keyboard navigation
- Include visible focus states
- Respect reduced motion
- Use semantic HTML
- Provide descriptive alt text
- Maintain strong color contrast

---

# Mobile Experience

On mobile:

- Collapse navigation into a hamburger menu.
- Stack hero content vertically.
- Keep CTA buttons full width.
- Convert roadmap into a vertical timeline.
- Maintain readable typography and spacing.
- Ensure all interactions are touch-friendly.

---

# Success Criteria

The landing page is successful if a first-time visitor can answer these questions within five seconds:

- What is this product?
- Who is it for?
- What problem does it solve?
- How is it different from existing legal research tools?
- What should I do next?

If those answers are clear without requiring extensive reading, the landing page has achieved its purpose.

---

# Landing Page Mission

> **The landing page should transform curiosity into confidence by immediately showing users that CaseCompass AI is not another legal search engine—it is a personalized guide that helps them understand where to begin.**
