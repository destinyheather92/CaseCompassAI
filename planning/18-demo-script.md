# 18. Demo Script

## Overview

This demo introduces **CaseCompass AI**, an AI-powered legal research roadmap platform designed to help incarcerated individuals better understand legal concepts, identify relevant research topics, and navigate the legal system through plain-language guidance.

**Important Disclaimer:** CaseCompass AI does **not** provide legal advice and does **not** replace licensed attorneys. The platform is an educational research assistant that helps users understand *where to begin* and *what to research*.

---

This demonstration is intended for:

# Expected Questions

### How is this different from ChatGPT?

CaseCompass is purpose-built for legal education. It uses guided intake, legal-specific prompts, retrieval from trusted legal sources, citation verification, and safeguards that reduce hallucinations and prevent users from mistaking educational content for legal advice.

---

### Does it replace attorneys?

No.

CaseCompass is an educational research assistant. It helps users understand legal concepts and identify research topics but does not provide legal advice or predict legal outcomes.

---

### How do you reduce AI hallucinations?

Through:

- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Citation verification
- Confidence scoring
- Prompt engineering
- AI validation pipeline
- Trusted legal sources

---

### Why is this needed?

Many incarcerated individuals have a legal right to access research materials but lack the legal literacy needed to understand them. CaseCompass helps bridge that educational gap by making legal information more understandable and approachable.
